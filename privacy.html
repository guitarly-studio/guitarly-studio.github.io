Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

At guitarly (G9: guitar apps), we are committed to providing high-quality tools for musicians while respecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data across all our mobile applications and websites.

No Data Collection

Backing Tracks, Guitar Solo - Backing Tracks, and guitarly Practice - Learn, do not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require accounts, we do not track your location, and we do not access your contacts.

Local Storage

Any data you create within the apps (such as chord progressions or practice history) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may contain links to the Google Play Store or our official website. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store developer contact info.
